About Meridian

Founded 2026 · New York

Meridian covers the convergence of traditional markets, digital assets and the frontier economy. Our differentiator is depth of record: an editorially reconstructed archive of every significant market event since Bitcoin's genesis block in January 2009 — so readers who join us today inherit seventeen years of context.

Editorial Standards

Independence · Accuracy · Correction

Meridian journalists do not hold positions in assets they cover. Every archive entry is verified against at least two primary sources. Errors are corrected prominently and logged publicly. Analysis is clearly labeled and never presented as investment advice.
